Profile of Maternal and Child Health Care Centre

Authored by Sister Bester Chamboko

üìå Overview

  • Established: August 1985, Zimbabwe.
  • Founder/Proprietor: Sister Bester Chamboko.
  • Mission: Deliver high-standard healthcare to mothers and children (mainly under age 5), especially working mothers, using an appointment-based system to avoid queues.

üè• Current Services

  • Well Baby Clinic: Includes immunisations (Zimbabwe Expanded Programme of Immunisations).
  • Well Woman Clinic: Offers family planning services.
  • Maternity Services: Includes antenatal and postnatal care.
  • Referrals: To local doctors and paediatricians when needed.
  • Parent-craft education (including relaxation exercises).
  • Postnatal and fitness classes for women.

  • Originally operated from small premises (Fort Street/14th Avenue).
  • Moved in 1992 to a larger property at 113A Josiah Tongogara Street, Bulawayo.
  • A 1996 survey confirmed client satisfaction and demand for on-site delivery services.
  • In 2002, original structure was demolished to allow for expansion into a three-story facility.

üåü Future Aspirations

Offer full maternity home services with overnight stays. Include:

  • Operating theatre for C-sections (in collaboration with Obstetricians and Paediatricians).
  • Special care unit for babies.
  • Capacity for up to 50 inpatients.
  • Employment opportunities for expanded staff.
