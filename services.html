Our Services

We Offer:

  • Prenatal care for the expectant mother.
  • Regular checkups.
  • Nutritional guidance.
  • Delivery of babies the right way, at the right time.
  • Monitoring for the growing child, administering immunizations and taking other necessary actions when due and /or when necessary.

From prenatal classes, through birth, to postnatal support, our team guides your every step, delivering professional care through every stage of motherhood and childhood.

  • Regular child wellness checks.
  • Well Baby Clinic

  • Parent-craft education (including relaxation exercises).
  • Postnatal and fitness classes for women.
